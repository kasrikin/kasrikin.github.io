About Me

I am a graduate-level educated Sound Designer, Audio Editor, and Technical Audio Specialist who has worked on over 30 films in sound editorial, sound mixing, and sound design for the past 8 years. I have collaborated with content creators, musicians, and other creatives to make their visions come to life through creative story-specific sound design and unique immersive audio techniques.

My main goal is to use my skills in sound editing and design with industry-standard software such as Pro Tools (Avid Certified User), Wwise (Audiokinetic Certified User) and my commitment to best practices to design high-quality sound products to the benefit of the client, creator, and consumer alike. My work has been featured in venues and screenings domestically and internationally such as the Cannes Film Festival, PBS, California Symphony, and the Saint Petersburg International Film Festival (SPIFF).

I am highly interested in new and disruptive technologies and their possible uses with audio and sound technology synthesis. I believe that the intersection of technology and storytelling is crucial to creating culturally significant advancements and messages.
